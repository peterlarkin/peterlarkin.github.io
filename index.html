About

Motivated by a desire to solve real-world problems, I bring a mix of strategic thinking, creative ideas and executional detail to help create work that's bold and meaningful. With a strong focus on digital design and coding, I've spent the last ten years working as an all-round creative, leading to my current role as Creative Director at purpose-led creative agency Nice and Serious.

During my career, I've helped build brands, digital products and award-winning campaigns that have played a part in shifting perceptions, raising money, driving action and changing policy.
